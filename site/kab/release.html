Anṣuf ar Thunderbird

Ttekki di Thunderbird

Akken ad neḍmen asnerni n Thunderbird, am akken yezga di mraw-agi n iseggassen ineggura, imttekkiyen urmiden mlalen tagara-agi akken ad xemmemen i uzekka ines. Tagnitt tewweḍ-d akken ad tebduḍ deg uttekki. Nesra-k akken ad nmudd afus i yiseqdacen n Thunderbird akked usnerni n useɣẓan Thunderbird akked twannaṭ n unagraw.

Nettnadi ad nsel sɣur-k.

Tesrid tallalt?
